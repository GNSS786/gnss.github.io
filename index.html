Co-Curricular Excellence

Sports Facilities

Work Experience, Art Education, Games & Sports. "A healthy mind lives in a healthy body." Regular physical activity improves fitness, reduces anxiety, builds strong bones.

Art & Cultural Hub

Music-Vocal & Instrumental, Dance (Folk, Classical, Western). Aesthetically designed dance & music rooms, Activity Block, sprawling grounds, expert coaching.

School Functions

Annual Prize Distribution, Sports Day, Investiture Ceremony, Founder Father’s Birthday, Republic Day, Independence Day, Teacher’s Day, Children’s Day, Exhibitions, School Fete — manifesting practical knowledge.

Tours & Excursions

Comprehensive schedule for tours/treks every year — learning through travelling, self-management. Regular tours, treks, educational trips at Rajasthan.

Competitions & Student Leadership

Inter-House & Inter-School

Regular inter-house competitions channel leadership potentials. Six houses named after eminent personalities. House tutors motivate participation.

Students’ Council

Head Boy, Head Girl, Sports Head, Prefects — selected on academic record, discipline, leadership. Apex body infuses self-reliance.

About Us - Legacy & Leadership

Our Founder & Management

Inspired by the values of Guru Nanak Dev Ji, our institution was founded with a vision to provide holistic education blending modern pedagogy with traditional ethics. The Chairperson’s message echoes commitment to excellence, and the Principal’s desk drives innovation.

Vision: To create responsible global citizens. Mission: Foster curiosity, resilience, and compassion. Values: Integrity, Respect, Excellence.

From the Desk of Principal

"Education is the most powerful weapon to change the world. At Guru Nanak School, we blend academic rigour with co-curricular vibrancy." – Principal
